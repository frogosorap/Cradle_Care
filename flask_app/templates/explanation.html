How it works

Ambient Light Sensor

The Ambient Light Sensor is a high-performance sensor designed to measure ambient light levels in lumens. This sensor can measure light levels from 0 to 200,000 lux (200klx), making it suitable for a variety of lighting conditions, from very low light to direct sunlight. Based on the setting the parent chooses , the device will have an acceptable threshold. It will check the light level of the room roughly every 3 minutes and based on it will adjust the curtains accordingly.

Sound sensor

The sound sensor will have a basic threshold, which can be further changed by the user. After that point, whenever the sensor detects a noise above that threshold it will send a notification to the users device indicating a loud noise was detected.
